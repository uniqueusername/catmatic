Hi! I'm catmatic. I make electronic music. I'm infatuated with virtual worlds and the way art, music, and interactive media can coalesce to immerse a player in an alien environment. Some of my favorite games, especially for their composition, are FEZ and Portal 2. I'm inspired by artists like Porter Robinson and Madeon, who take listeners on journeys. I put some demos below that I hope you'll like. If you do, don't hesitate to contact me via the contact page or email. Thanks for listening!
