Hi! My name is catmatic. I produce electronic music for soundtracks and for myself. As a musician, I aim to create music that both reflects what I feel, and helps the listener feel the same way. Some of my biggest inspirations are Porter Robinson and Madeon, both amazing artists whom I aspire to live up to some day. I have prior experience working on video game soundtracks, having worked on the Automaton project on GameJolt and Royal Rumble, a choose-your-own-adventure game developed for the CodeDay hackathon. I hope you enjoy my music and thanks for listening (the buttons switch between playlists)!
